Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (October to March). This ensures availability and gives us time to plan your perfect day.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes! We love destination weddings and travel across India and internationally. Travel costs are additional and discussed during consultation.

How long does it take to receive our photos/videos?

You'll receive a sneak peek within 48 hours. Complete edited photos are delivered within 4-6 weeks, and cinematic films within 8-10 weeks.

Can we customize our package?

Absolutely! Every couple is unique, and we tailor our services to match your specific needs, style, and budget. Let's discuss your vision!
